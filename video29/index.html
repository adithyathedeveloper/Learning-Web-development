Vellore Fort

Vellore Fort is a tourist location present at vellore as the name states, it exactly finds itself opposite to the old bus stand. This fort is considered to have a lake surrounding the entire four side compound of the fort. It is said that crocoiles were present in the past inside the lake in the form of security to the fort, the only way to enter the fort would be by the bridge which can be opened and closed as per the needs. The speciality of the fort is that it contains Temple, Church and also a Mosque promoting religious tolerance.
